Mootie

This video was made for a Nigerian clothing brand called Mootie. The aim was to guide international Clients through the process of getting their measurements. This Video was made on after effects, and utilized illustrations I made in Illustrator to guide the clients.

Nationhood

During the most recent World Cup tournament, a friend and I began to work on a brand called Nationhood. The brand was created to inspire hope for the future of our country(Nigeria). In order to this I worked on shirt designs and a video which chanelled the energy of the World Cup to something positive.
