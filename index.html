MY PORTFOLIO

I am a Nigerian Born Visual Artist. I work with a variety of mediums, and have a passion for diffrent forms of art ranging from painting to filmmaking. I am currently majoring in communication design, which includes sub sections such as graphic and interface design in Parsons school of design. I am also minoring in film production. My influence in art has always been from my father, and the people he works with, many of which are artists in the advertising industry. My major source of inspiration is history, and my love for history pushes me to create works with meaning.

I personally believe in creating works that in one way or another engages the mind. Ultimately art is a form of expression and I am striving to become versitile enough to best express myself through my body of works. Bellow are projects I have worked on divided into different categories
