MY PORTFOLIO

I am a Nigerian born visual artist. I work with a variety of mediums, and have a passion for diffrent forms of art ranging from painting to filmmaking. I am currently majoring in communication design, which includes sub sections such as graphic and interface design in Parsons, New York. I also minor in film production. My influence in art has always been from my father and the people he works with, many of which are artists in the advertising industry.

I personally believe in creating works that in one way or another engages the mind. Ultimately art is a form of expression and I am striving to become versitile enough to best express myself through my body of works. Bellow are projects I have worked on divided into different categories.
